Little tools for being a better friend

Ghostie watches your back so you stay close to people, and turns your texting life into something you actually want to look at. All on your Mac. Pick your favorites, ignore the rest.

Don't Ghost

The conversations that have gone quiet: the ones waiting on your reply, and the ones worth reaching out on first. Sorted by who you'd hate to lose touch with. No guilt trips, just a nudge.

Texting Wrapped

Your year in texts as story cards. Equal parts flattering and incriminating.

Texting Analytics

Who texts first? Who double-texts? The receipts, charted, for your eyes only.

EQ

A gentle read on your relationships, with little nudges to show up better for the people in them: friends, partner, siblings, all of it.

Drafts

A calm place to write a text out and sit on it, instead of firing off something at 2am you'll regret by breakfast.

Scheduled

Line up a text for later, poke yourself to call Mom on Sunday, or set a standing "rent's due" nudge for your roommate.

Work / Personal

Files work threads away from life threads, Severance-mode easter egg included. Your innie would approve.

Want Ghostie to actually ghostwrite? Hand the keys to Claude or Codex and it'll draft replies in your voice, every one still waiting for your approval. That part's for the tinkerers.
