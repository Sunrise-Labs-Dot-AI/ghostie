Four ways the app could look.

Same three surfaces, same real copy, grounded in research on the newest macOS app design languages. Click any direction to see it full-size and watch the hold-to-send gesture animate. Pick one as the base, or tell me what to borrow from another.

Surfaces in each: Messages (the approval gate) · Settings (nested) · Home (status). Hero interaction: press & hold to approve a send.

Anchor · Neo-skeuomorphism

Tactile

Warm molded material. The draft is a physical key you press down into the surface to approve — the embodied act of saying “yes, send it.”
