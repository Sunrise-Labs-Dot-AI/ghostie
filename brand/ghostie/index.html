01 Logo and wordmark

Soft, legible, never spooky.

The mark combines a ghost, a speech bubble, and a tiny spark of care. The wordmark is round and warm, with enough polish for a macOS app and enough charm for Wrapped cards.

Primary lockup for marketing headers, press, onboarding, and brand introductions.

Standalone mark for menu bar, small UI, social avatars, and favicon contexts.

Wordmark for wide layouts where the mascot appears elsewhere in the composition.

macOS app icon: mint and honey field, ink outline, clear face at dock size.

Construction note

  • Use a 96 by 96 grid. The body sits from x 9 to 78 and y 13 to 79.
  • Keep the outline at 3 px, rounded caps, rounded joins, and the same bottom scallop rhythm.
  • Eyes are round, dark, and spaced on a calm centerline. Cheeks are coral at low opacity.
  • Only expressions, arms, and small props change. The body, cheeks, and outline stay consistent.
  • The ghost is a helper, not a prank. Avoid sharp teeth, horror cues, smoke trails, or cluttered props.
