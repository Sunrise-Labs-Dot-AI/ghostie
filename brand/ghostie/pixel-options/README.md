# Ghostie Relationship Ops Direction

Ghostie is now framed as the tiny private back office for your texts.

The brand is no longer just pseudo-8bit mascot exploration. The selected direction is generated raster scene art: Ghostie has little rooms for the jobs he does. The office floor handles quiet threads, automations, and power mode. The therapy chair handles EQ, tone, and delicate replies. The chalkboard handles analytics, Wrapped, and texting patterns.

## Brand Idea

**Do not ghost. Let Ghostie ghostwrite.**

Ghostie does the hidden work behind better replies: tracking quiet threads, reading emotional tone, drafting in your voice, remembering people, and finding patterns in your texting life. He always waits for approval.

## Visual Direction

- Soft office surreal, built for Mac.
- Generated raster art, pixel-dense but not true low-res pixel art.
- Warm paper UI with occasional odd little office-world scenes.
- Calm product surfaces, expressive Ghostie cameos.
- Consumer door feels warm, emotionally literate, and friendly.
- Power door feels darker, quieter, and operator-like.

## Type Direction

- Recommended display: `Bricolage Grotesque`
- Recommended product copy: `Atkinson Hyperlegible`
- Recommended labels and code-like controls: `Recursive`
- Softer consumer alternate: `Fraunces` display, `Literata` body, `Spline Sans Mono` labels.
- Power utility alternate: `Space Grotesk` display, `Atkinson Hyperlegible` body, `Spline Sans Mono` labels.
- Friendly product alternate: `Gabarito` display, `IBM Plex Sans` body, `Recursive` labels.
- Private journal alternate: `Newsreader` display, `IBM Plex Sans` body, `IBM Plex Mono` labels.
- Office bureau alternate: `Chivo` display, `IBM Plex Sans` body, `Azeret Mono` labels.
- The display type should be legible first. The raster character carries the pixel personality.

## App Icon Direction

- Use the clean Ghostie cutout for the classic macOS app mark.
- Ghostie should be large enough to read at dock size.
- Avoid construction-grid remnants in the app icon background.
- `Clean` is the simplest default: large Ghostie, soft paper tile, no extra story.
- `Soft Tile` adds depth while staying character-first.
- `Message`, `Approval`, `Console`, and `Utility` test product cues around the same clean silhouette.
- Avoid readable text inside the app icon.

## Character Rules

- Generate a new Ghostie scene per use case.
- Keep shared Ghostie DNA: near-white body, chunky black outline, blue-gray side shadow, block feet, rectangular eyes, short mouth vocabulary.
- Do not force one exact body silhouette into every feature.
- Props should feel integrated into the room or scene, not pasted on top.
- No construction grid, no labels, no logos, no readable in-image copy.
- Keep Ghostie a ghost, not a human, robot, sea creature, or white logo blob.

## Route Guidance

- Lead consumer marketing with the therapy and friend-care side: EQ, birthdays, quiet threads, better replies.
- Lead power marketing with the office and analytics side: BYOK, rules, automation, message intelligence.
- Keep both doors connected through the same core idea: Ghostie does the invisible relationship work, then asks before anything sends.
- Use one calm master macOS shell for navigation, settings, account state, approvals, and global queues.
- Let individual tool tabs keep their own room interiors: therapy for EQ, chalkboard for analytics, office floor for automations and power tools.
