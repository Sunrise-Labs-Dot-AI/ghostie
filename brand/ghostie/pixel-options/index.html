Selected direction: relationship ops in miniature

The back office for your texts.

Ghostie is a tiny generated-raster character who does the hidden work behind better replies: tracking quiet threads, reading tone, drafting in your voice, and finding the patterns in your texting life. Warm enough for friends, weird enough for power users.

00 Brand Idea

Do not ghost. Let Ghostie ghostwrite.

The brand behaves like a tiny private office inside your Mac. Ghostie is not a sticker on the UI. He is the character doing the work, checking the inbox, sitting with the feelings, running the math, then waiting for approval.

01

Private by default

Local message context is the world. The UI should feel tucked-away, personal, and trusted.

02

Emotionally literate

Ghostie reads the room before he writes the line. Funny, soft, precise.

03

Approval-gated

Ghostie drafts, you decide. The product is helpful without ever grabbing the wheel.

04

Playfully competent

The character can be odd and expressive while the app still feels sharp.

02 Character World

Ghostie has rooms for the work he does.

The new character direction is generated scene art, not one pose with props. Shared Ghostie DNA, different job, different expression, different tiny room.

The Office Floor

Quiet threads, automations, power mode.

Fluorescent teal, cubicles, old terminals, tiny bureaucracy. This is where Ghostie watches the queue and keeps the system honest.

The Therapy Chair

Tone check, EQ, delicate replies.

Warm lamp, sage chair, lavender scarf, patient little notepad. This is the consumer heart of the brand.

The Chalkboard

Analytics, Wrapped, texting patterns.

Charts, formulas, message bubbles, and a tiny overwhelmed analyst. Smart, dense, and still cute.

Art Rules

  • Generate a new Ghostie scene per use case.
  • Keep the body near-white with a chunky black outline.
  • Use props as part of the room, not stickers on top.
  • No construction grid, no labels, no logos.

05 Two Front Doors

Same app, two dials.

The consumer door leads with care and warmth. The power door reveals the office machinery: BYOK, automation, analysis, and approval controls.

Consumer Door

Better friend mode

Messaging as care, not productivity theater. Ghostie feels like a tiny therapist, birthday buddy, and draft helper who keeps you from vanishing.

  • Lead with EQ, birthdays, quiet-thread nudges.
  • Use warm paper, sage, lavender, coral.
  • Copy is candid, apologetic, and kind.

Power Door

Operator mode

Message intelligence for people who want the machinery exposed. Ghostie becomes analyst, operator, and local automation clerk.

  • Lead with BYOK, rules, analysis, approval.
  • Use deep green, console mint, cyan, black.
  • Copy is precise, dry, and a little uncanny.
